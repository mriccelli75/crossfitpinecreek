We are a community of real people.

Third Space Fitness was established based on the concept that for humans to truly thrive there is a need to exist within a social space. A place outside of work and home where one can be relieved of responsibility and stress. A place where, for one hour a day, one can focus on his or her own pursuits both mentally and physically.

What is Crossfit?

CrossFit is constantly varied functional movements performed at high intensity. All CrossFit workouts are based on functional movements, and these movements reflect the best aspects of gymnastics, weightlifting, running, rowing and more. These are the core movements of life.

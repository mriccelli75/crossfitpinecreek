About Us Here at Crossfit Pine Creek.

Third Space Fitness is the home of CrossFit Pine Creek. We are located in the North Hills of Pittsburgh, bordering the communities of Millvale and Shaler. We are easily accessible, located less than 15 minutes north of downtown Pittsburgh with convenient access to Babcock Blvd, McKnight Road and Route 8. Our facility is light, bright and ready for activities. With over 3,000 square feet of premium fitness space, our facility also boasts two bathrooms (with showers), a partitioned space for children, athlete lounge area and plenty of parking. Our goal is to provide a safe, clean and well equipped gym space for the training needs of our members, families and the community.

Our Team

The coaching team at CrossFit Pine Creek brings a varied mix of coaching types, skills/certifications and interests. This diversity allows us to provide exactly what you need. If your interest is fine tuning your nutrition, become stronger, improve general fitness, or excel at team sports we can help. We continue to expand our knowledge, by educating ourselves, attending certifications and seminars and striving for continuous improvement.

Dameka Dubuc

CrossFit Level 2 trainer, CrossFit Kids - Owner / Head Coach

Dameka’s professional background is in Community Health programming and evaluation.

My fitness journey started like many of you, at a time I was run down, overweight and depressed. I had never been all that athletic or involved in sports growing up, but I knew I needed a change. Over the years I learned about sustainable nutrition, and fitness became my passion. Starting off with running in 2010 and later in 2012 I was introduced to CrossFit and my idea of fitness was forever changed. I believe strongly Greg Glassman’s statement, “the needs of Olympic athletes and our grandparents differ by degree, not kind “ We all benefit from moving well and with good mechanics, developing strength, agility, coordination and all the other aspects of Fitness. At the end of 2013 I received my CrossFit Level 1 certification and in 2014 I received my CrossFit kids certification and began coaching adults, teens and children at CrossFit Iron City. I enjoy expressing my fitness by competing in local competitions as a masters athlete.

In 2018, I obtained my CrossFit Level 2 trainer certification. It was at that time the dream of opening my own facility was born. I envisioned a clean, family friendly space open to the community with the goal to support health and fitness habits across the lifespan. A place that is broad, inclusive, welcoming and supportive. We know how hard it is to start, but once you start we hope you’ll never leave.

Nick Spagnol

Certifications: Crossfit - L-1 Trainer
Crossfit - Spot the Flaw

I grew up playing sports and continued to do so through high school. After high school I spent my time lifting weights and running to fulfill my fitness needs. Over time I began to feel like my workout routine was growing stagnant and I needed a change, so I decided to try Crossfit.

I did my first Crossfit workout on November 2, 2016 and I fell in love instantly. I obtained my L-1 Certificate to Coach CrossFit in September 2018 and I began coaching in November 2018. While I don’t expect everyone to instantly fall in love with Crossfit like I did, I know that my passion and knowledge of the Crossfit methodology allows me to be an effective coach who enjoys helping people change their lives using CrossFit.

My goal as a coach is to help our athletes move safely and effectively while improving the quality of their lives. I enjoy the process of seeing athletes grow and progress both physically and mentally using the Crossfit methodology. I truly believe that Crossfit is for anyone of any age and background and I look forward to providing athletes with the best hour of their day each time they walk into our gym.
